## What Open Paws Does

Open Paws builds AI tools for animal advocacy. The tech stack includes:
- **Prediction models** for advocacy campaign outcomes
- **Knowledge graphs and vector databases** for advocacy resources
- **Speciesist framing metrics** for measuring language bias in AI systems
- **Structured coding instruction sets** for 12+ AI coding tools (137 files)
- **Project management tools** (CryptPad fork with strategic project scoring)

The organization operates globally with contributors across multiple countries, maintaining a fully open-source approach to everything.

## The Path to Local Director

### Why India Needs a Local Entity
Open Paws operates globally but needed an Indian legal entity for:
- **Payment processing:** Indian banking regulations require a local entity for receiving and disbursing funds
- **Hiring:** Employing Indian developers (like the 20 Adventurers Guild interns) requires a registered company
- **Tax compliance:** GST registration, TDS compliance, and proper invoicing
- **Partnership legitimacy:** Indian NGOs, government bodies, and funders prefer working with registered entities

### Open Paws India Pvt Ltd
Currently being registered with:
- **Open Paws founder** as primary shareholder and director
- **Me (Abid Khan)** as second shareholder and Local Director
- Operating as the Indian arm of the global Open Paws organization
- Handling all India-based operations: payments, hiring, partnerships, compliance

### What Local Director Means
Not a ceremonial title. The role covers:
- **Operations:** Managing Indian team members, intern payments, vendor relationships
- **Compliance:** Ensuring the entity meets Indian regulatory requirements
- **Strategy:** Adapting global Open Paws priorities to Indian market context
- **Business development:** Indian-specific partnerships, hackathon organizing, campus programs
