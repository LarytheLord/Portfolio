AFOG: Democratizing Legal Advocacy

From 10-hour hackathon project to production-ready civic infrastructure - building AI that democratizes legal advocacy.

Civic Tech AI Automation Legal Tech

The Origin Story

Born at the Mumbai Code 4 Compassion hackathon, AFOG started as a 10-hour prototype to solve a critical problem: communities losing battles against factory farms due to a lack of legal resources. We set out to do in seconds what lawyers take weeks to accomplish.

The 100x Scale

For the finals, we upgraded from a proof-of-concept to production. We assembled a team across 3 continents (India, USA, Canada) to build features like auto-detection of permits, real legal citations, and submission tracking. Our target was to generate 50 real objection letters from actual permits.

Technical Architecture

AI & NLP

Gemini API integration for legal text generation and NLP processing.

Backend

Python backend managing a legal citation library and permit tracking system.

Frontend

Node.js/React frontend providing an intuitive chatbot infrastructure.

The system includes an automation pipeline for permit filing detection, ensuring timely responses to new applications.

Social Impact

AFOG democratizes legal advocacy for under-resourced communities through an open-source civic tech approach. Partnering with NGOs and animal welfare organizations, we created a scalable tool with infinite use cases.

Remote Team Dynamics

Coordinating across 12-hour time zones required strict async collaboration best practices. We successfully balanced in-person hackathon participants with remote contributors to deliver a polished product.
