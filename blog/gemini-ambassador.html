The Ambassador Mission

My role serves as a bridge between Google AI and student developers, with the goal of democratizing AI education on campus through workshops, hackathons, and office hours.

Workshop Highlights

We focused on AI integration and terminal automation. Students got hands-on experience building with the Gemini API, creating CLI tools, chatbots, and workflow automation scripts. We covered prompt engineering and practical API usage.

Community Building

We created spaces for AI experimentation, supported hackathons with mentorship, and connected students with industry opportunities, fostering a strong peer learning culture.

Impact Stories

We've seen students ship projects and gain valuable skills. Campus AI adoption has increased, and we're constantly iterating on our workshop formats based on feedback.

Future Plans

We plan to expand workshop topics to include advanced prompt engineering and multimodal AI, host industry collaboration events, and build a lasting student AI community.
